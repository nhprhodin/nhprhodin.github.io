Nils Henrik Pehlivan Rhodin

I am a Ph.D. student in the extragalactic group lead by Lise Christensen at the Dark Cosmology Centre of the Niels Bohr Institute, Denmark. My research concerns the detection and analysis of high-redshift galaxies. In 2015 I received my Masters degree in Astronomy from Lund Observatory, Sweden. I have a deep appreciation for the clarity, open source and reproducibility in my proffesional work.

My interests include:

  • Galaxy formation and evolution
  • Data reduction, analysis and visualization
  • The scientific method in theory and practise
